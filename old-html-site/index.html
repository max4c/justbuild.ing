Frequently Asked Questions +

Can anyone join?

Yes – no credentials required. We welcome all passionate builders.

Who is behind this?

JustBuild was founded and is run by Max Forsey and Jacob Wright.

How are winners selected for hackathons?

Criteria differ for each hackathon, so please check the specific event page for details. In general, we value innovation, technical ability, and creativity. A great demo goes a long way!

Where can I learn about coworking sessions?

Our regular coworking sessions are called saturdays@byu. Check the Event Calendar for the schedule and location details.

Do I need a company to apply/participate?

No, you do not need a company to join the community or participate in events.

I love this idea and I want to help! Can I provide additional funding, sponsorship, a venue, or mentorship?

Yes! Thank you for being awesome! If you want to contribute in any way, please email us at hello@justbuild.ing or join the slack and DM Max or Jacob.

I have further questions! How can I reach you?

You can reach us at hello@justbuild.ing
