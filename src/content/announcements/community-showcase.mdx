# January Community Showcase & Networking

We're kicking off 2024 with our first community showcase and networking event on January 18th. This is a chance to share what you're building, get feedback from peers, and forge connections with other builders in Utah County.

## Event Format

Unlike our hackathons, this event is designed for sharing work-in-progress or completed projects in a casual setting:

### Lightning Demos (6:00-7:30 PM)

Each participant will have 5 minutes to demo their project, followed by 3 minutes of Q&A. The goal is to share what you're working on and get constructive feedback.

You can present:
- Side projects
- Startups
- Open source contributions
- Research projects
- Interesting technical challenges you've solved

### Structured Networking (7:30-9:00 PM)

After the demos, we'll facilitate meaningful connections through guided networking activities designed to help you find potential collaborators, mentors, or teammates for future projects.

## Who Should Attend

This event is perfect for:

- Developers looking for feedback on their projects
- Founders seeking technical collaborators
- People interested in joining project teams
- Anyone who wants to connect with the Utah tech community

## Registration

Space is limited to 30 participants for demos (first come, first served) and 50 attendees total.

When registering, please indicate:
1. Whether you plan to demo a project
2. A brief description of your project (if applicable)
3. What kind of connections you're hoping to make

## Location & Details

The event will be held at DevMountain's campus in Provo from 6:00-9:00 PM.

Light refreshments will be provided, with time for casual networking after the structured activities.

## Why Attend?

- **Get valuable feedback** on your projects from fellow builders
- **Find collaborators** for your next venture
- **Discover interesting projects** happening in the Utah tech scene
- **Build meaningful connections** with like-minded builders

## Questions?

If you have any questions about the event, please email us at [hello@justbuild.ing](mailto:hello@justbuild.ing).

We look forward to seeing what everyone is building!
