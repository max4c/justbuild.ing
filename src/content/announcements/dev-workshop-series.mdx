# Advanced Development Workshop Series

We're launching a new workshop series designed to help developers in our community level up specific technical skills. The series will run on consecutive Saturdays in February 2024.

## Workshop Schedule

### Workshop 1: Practical AI Integration
**February 3, 2024 | 10:00 AM - 2:00 PM | Lehi Tech Hub**

Learn how to effectively integrate AI capabilities into your applications using modern APIs and open-source models. This hands-on workshop will cover:

- Choosing the right AI models for your use case
- Optimizing prompts for better results
- Fine-tuning models with your own data
- Building responsible AI systems with proper safeguards

**Instructor:** Dr. Michelle Park, AI Research Scientist at Anthropic

---

### Workshop 2: System Design for Scale
**February 10, 2024 | 10:00 AM - 2:00 PM | Provo Innovation Center**

This workshop will walk you through designing systems that can scale to millions of users while maintaining performance and reliability:

- Database selection and optimization strategies
- Caching approaches and implementation
- Load balancing and horizontal scaling
- Designing for fault tolerance

**Instructor:** James Martinez, Senior Staff Engineer at Stripe

---

### Workshop 3: Frontend Performance Optimization
**February 17, 2024 | 10:00 AM - 2:00 PM | Lehi Tech Hub**

Learn how to build lightning-fast web applications through advanced performance optimization techniques:

- Core Web Vitals optimization
- Bundle size reduction strategies
- Runtime performance tuning
- Advanced rendering patterns

**Instructor:** Eliza Thompson, Frontend Architect at Vercel

---

### Workshop 4: Build Your Own SaaS in a Weekend
**February 24, 2024 | 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM | DevMountain Campus**

An extended workshop where you'll build and deploy a complete SaaS application in a single day:

- Setting up authentication and billing
- Building a core feature set
- Implementing analytics
- Preparing for launch

**Instructors:** Jacob Wright and Max Forsey, justbuild.ing co-founders

## Registration Details

Each workshop costs $75, or you can register for the full series for $250 (saving $50).

All workshops include:
- Hands-on instruction
- Workshop materials
- Lunch and refreshments
- Access to a private Discord channel for continued learning

## Requirements

Participants should:
- Bring their own laptop
- Have intermediate to advanced programming experience
- Complete pre-workshop setup instructions (to be sent before each session)

## Limited Seats

Each workshop is limited to 20 participants to ensure a high-quality, hands-on experience. Register early to secure your spot.

Registration opens December 20th at 12:00 PM MST. A registration link will be posted on our website and sent to our mailing list.
