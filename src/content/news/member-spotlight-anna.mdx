# Member Spotlight: Anna Chen

*Each month, we highlight a member of the justbuild.ing community who's making waves with their projects and contributions. This month, we're featuring Anna Chen, founder of Memocraft.*

## Background

Anna Chen is a full-stack developer based in Provo who joined our community six months ago. With a background in computer science from the University of Utah and previous experience at Adobe, Anna brings valuable expertise in user experience and frontend development.

## The Project: Memocraft

Anna's current focus is Memocraft, a note-taking application that uses machine learning to automatically organize and connect related ideas across a user's notes.

"I've always been frustrated with how note-taking apps work in silos," says Anna. "Our brains don't compartmentalize information that way—we make connections between concepts all the time. Memocraft tries to mimic that natural thought process."

The app has gained over 10,000 users since its beta launch three months ago, with a particularly strong following among researchers, writers, and students.

## Technical Challenges

Building Memocraft wasn't without challenges. Anna describes the difficulty of creating an algorithm that could understand context and relevance across different types of notes:

"We had to develop a custom embedding model that understands not just keywords, but concepts and their relationships. The breakthrough came when we incorporated user feedback into the training data—the system now learns from how users manually connect their notes."

## Community Impact

Anna credits the justbuild.ing community with helping refine early versions of Memocraft.

"The feedback I received during our hackathon was invaluable. Several community members became early beta testers and helped identify issues I would have missed. Plus, connecting with others who are passionate about building things has kept me motivated during the difficult phases of development."

## What's Next

Anna is working on the next version of Memocraft, which will include collaborative features and an API for third-party integrations.

"I'm excited about turning Memocraft from a personal tool into a platform that teams can use to build their collective knowledge base."
